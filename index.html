Getting Started with Trezor

Welcome to Trezor! Follow these steps to set up your Trezor hardware wallet and start securing your cryptocurrencies.

  1. Unbox your Trezor device.
  2. Connect your Trezor to your computer using the provided USB cable.
  3. Go to trezor.io/start to download the Trezor Bridge software.
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to set up your Trezor device.
  5. Securely store your recovery seed.
  6. Start using your Trezor to manage your cryptocurrencies.
